About Shoe Shiner Deodorant

Why Your Shoes Need Deodorant

Shoe odor is caused by bacteria and moisture buildup inside your shoes. Regular use of Shoe Shiner deodorant:

  • Controls moisture: Reduces moisture that bacteria thrive in
  • Eliminates bacteria: Fights odor-causing bacteria effectively
  • Maintains shoe hygiene: Keeps shoes fresh between washes
  • Extends shoe life: Prevents bacterial damage to materials

How to Use

Step 1: Remove shoes and allow to air dry for a few minutes

Step 2: Use the sponge applicator to apply deodorant inside the shoe

Step 3: Focus on insoles and inner surfaces where odor forms

Step 4: Leave shoes to dry for 10-15 minutes before wearing

Step 5: Use 2-3 times per week for best results
