✦ Our Journey

Born in the Streets of Delhi

Shoe Shiner was founded with one simple goal: to provide affordable, premium-quality shoe care products to every Indian household. Based out of Sarai Kale Khan, New Delhi, we have been serving customers with dedication and honest service.

Our multi-purpose shoe polish formula has been carefully developed to work on all types of footwear — from everyday canvas shoes to premium leather formals. We believe that well-maintained shoes speak volumes about a person.

With thousands of happy customers across Delhi NCR and growing, Shoe Shiner has become the trusted name for shoe care in the region.
