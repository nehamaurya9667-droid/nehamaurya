✅ Returns & Exchange Policy

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our products. If you're not completely satisfied, we make returns easy:

Return Eligibility

  • Products must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Product should be in original, unused condition
  • Packaging and labels must be intact
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation) required

Return Process

Step 1: Contact us on WhatsApp with order details and reason for return

Step 2: We provide return instructions and pickup details

Step 3: Package the product securely in original packaging

Step 4: Hand over to our representative during pickup

Step 5: Refund processed within 3-5 business days after verification

Exchange Policy

Don't like the product? Exchange for another:

  • Valid for 7 days from delivery date
  • Product must be in original, unused condition
  • Exchange for same or different product available
  • Price difference adjusted accordingly
  • No additional charges for exchange within Delhi

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Products showing signs of use or wear
  • Damaged packaging without proper claim within 24 hours
  • Items returned after 7 days of delivery
  • Bulk/wholesale orders (custom policy applies)
📞 Quick Support: WhatsApp us immediately if you receive a damaged product. We'll replace it right away!
