✦ Maintenance Guide

Complete Shoe Care Checklist

Daily, weekly, and monthly maintenance checklist to keep your shoes in perfect condition

Daily Shoe Care (After Wearing)

Remove & Air Dry

Take off shoes and let them air dry at room temperature for a few hours

Wipe Down Surface

Use a dry, soft cloth to wipe dirt and dust from the shoe surface

Check for Damage

Inspect shoes for scuffs, scratches, or signs of wear that need attention

Store Properly

Place shoes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat

Weekly Shoe Maintenance

Deep Clean

Use a soft brush and damp cloth to remove stubborn dirt and stains

Apply Polish

Use Shoe Shiner polish with the sponge applicator for shine and protection

Buff to Shine

Use a soft cloth or brush to buff and bring out the natural gleam

Check Heel & Sole

Inspect heel wear and sole condition for any repairs needed

Use Shoe Trees

Insert shoe trees to maintain shape and prevent creasing when stored

Monthly Deep Maintenance

Professional Clean

Perform a thorough clean with specialized cleaning solution if needed

Condition Leather

Use conditioning polish to nourish leather and maintain suppleness

Deep Shine Treatment

Apply extra polish layers for deep, mirror-like shine

Inspect Laces & Trim

Check condition of shoelaces, buckles, and any metal accessories

Rotate Shoes

Ensure you're rotating between multiple pairs for longevity

Seasonal Care (Before Each Season)

Monsoon Prep

Apply water-resistant polish before rainy season starts

Summer Protection

Store shoes away from direct sunlight to prevent discoloration

Humidity Control

Use desiccants or proper ventilation to manage moisture

Dust Protection

Polish regularly to maintain protective barrier against dust and pollution

Shoe Maintenance Timeline (Per Year)

Weekly: Polish and buff for shine
Monthly: Deep clean and condition
Quarterly: Comprehensive maintenance review
Seasonally: Weather-specific care adjustments
Annually: Professional inspection and repair if needed
